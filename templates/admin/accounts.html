How Account Assignment Works
  • When users make their first deposit, they are automatically assigned an available Google Ads account
  • The deposit amount (minus 5% fee) becomes their ad credit for that account
  • Subsequent deposits add more ad credit to their existing account
  • Accounts marked as "Assigned" cannot be removed from the system
  • Make sure to maintain a pool of available accounts for new users
